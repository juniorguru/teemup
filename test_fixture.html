  • Thu, Sep 7, 2023, 6:00 PM CEST [EN] ReactGirls & Jigx meetup
    Pipedrive, Praha-Praha 8

    Prague frontend meetup of ReactGirls community.

    🇬🇧 This meetup will be held in English.

    When: September 7th, from 6pm

    Where: offices Pipedrive , Pernerova 697/35, 186 00 Karlín

    Timetable:

    🕕 18.00 opening

    🎙 18.30 Talk 1:

    CONNECTING DOTS IN DIGITAL BUSINESS

    Filip Holický, Frontend Developer, Jigx

    Join us as we navigate the process of converting dreams into tangible business successes. Learn how the power of React and other digital tools can turn your innovative ideas into value-driven solutions. More than just profits, we'll explore how a digital business is about creating meaningful impact and truly helping people. Whether you dream of launching a startup or reshaping the digital landscape, discover how to connect the dots to create lasting opportunities. Dive into the world of digital business where purpose meets innovation.

    Filip, with a background in economics and business, has always been passionate about creating. His love for artistry steered him towards the dynamic realm of IT. As a developer, he seamlessly marries function with beauty in each digital solution, consistently aiming to provide a delightful experience for users.

    🎙 19:00 Talk 2:

    PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATION IN REACT

    Matúš König, Frontend Engineer, Mews

    Every application reaches a point, where it can't do any faster. We will disscuss common code weak points and real-life misconceptions about performance. We will learn simple ways to identify and fix performance pitfals and write fast code from the first keystroke. Enjoy the deep dive into fluent web applications, fast codebases and a world of endless optimalization.

    Matúš is a frontend engineer at Mews working at Reservations team. While they are a product team, performance optimalization and scalability is his thing for quite a time. He got a passion into frontend when He was of a young age and it never left him since. He is also a student of Charles university interested in AI.

    🎙 19.30 Talk 3:

    WILL IT RERENDER

    Miguel Jacinto, Frontend Developer, Pipedrive

    A look into when and why react rerenders and some tricks to avoid it.

    Miguel is portuguese but he has been living in Prague for 2 years. Have been working as a developer for around 9 years, started as a fullstack PHP developer and started working in pipedrive almost 5 years ago which is when he started working with frontend and react too.

    20.00 Networking

    ℹ️ We regularly take photos and videos at our events. We use photos and videos to promote our activities on the web and on social media. If you don't want to be in the photos, let the on-site organizers know.

    We look forward to it!

    ReactGirls team
